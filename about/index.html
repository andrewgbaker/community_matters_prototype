If it matters to you, it matters to us

As a community bank there’s one simple philosophy that has guided us throughout our 70 plus years in the business, ‘Hire good people, to run good banks, in good towns.’ Even today, each market has it’s own President and team to manage their community bank. This autonomy gives each market a bank that’s responsive to the needs of the community. A focus on what the community wants. Not on what we want to give them. That’s our heritage. That’s our path to the future. And that’s the way banking should be.
